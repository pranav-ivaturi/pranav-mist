Why Cats Are Amazing

Felines, from the domestic house cat to the mighty lion, are creatures of grace, mystery, and comfort. They have been companions to humans for millennia, providing cuddles, amusement, and a quiet, judging presence. This site is dedicated to celebrating the majesty of *Felis catus*.

Amazing Cat Facts

Sleep Masters

Cats spend approximately 70% of their lives asleep. That's about 16 hours a day dedicated to napping and dreaming of mice.

Unique Noses

A cat's nose pad is unique, much like a human fingerprint. No two cats have the exact same pattern!

Supersonic Hearing

Cats can hear frequencies up to 64 kHz, which is significantly higher than humans (20 kHz) and even dogs (45 kHz).

Liquid Bodies

Due to their highly flexible skeletons, cats can squeeze into nearly any container, leading to the popular theory that they are liquid.
